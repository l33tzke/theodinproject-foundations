Sagu de Vinho

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of sagu
  • 4 cups of red wine
  • sugar
  • 6 cups of water

How to prepare

  1. Place the sagu in a bowl with the water and let it soak for 15 minutes.
  2. Then drain the water from the sagu and put it to boil for about 10 to 15 minutes with just water, always stirring.
  3. Given these 15 minutes or as soon as some of the marbles start to become transparent
  4. Pour the contents of the pan into a pasta colander and wash them to remove the resulting cooking goo.
  5. While it is dripping, put the wine, sugar and a cup of water in the pan.
  6. When it starts to boil, add the sagu and cook for another 15 minutes, until all the balls are transparent.
  7. Put it in a container and let it cool.
